# Why Most Productivity Advice Fails

Productivity advice is everywhere, yet most people feel more overwhelmed than ever.
The problem isn’t effort; it’s misaligned incentives and unrealistic systems.

Most advice assumes infinite motivation.
In reality, motivation fluctuates and systems must absorb that variance.

Another issue is tool overload.
People adopt too many tools instead of simplifying their workflows.

The most effective productivity systems are boring.
They focus on reducing decisions, not optimizing every action.

Finally, productivity should serve life - not replace it.
If your system makes you miserable, it’s failing.
